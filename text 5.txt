# Syrian Seafarers Representation Website

**This repository contains the official files for the Syrian Seafarers Representation Project**, an independent initiative led by Mhmad Mostafa Mansour, MNI member and certified Second Officer.

## 🌍 Website

Live at: [https://manoboni.github.io/syrian-seafarers-org/](https://manoboni.github.io/syrian-seafarers-org/)

## 🧭 Mission

To represent, support, and empower Syrian maritime professionals, and connect them with international organizations for legal protection, rights advocacy, and professional growth.

## 📁 Files

- `index.html`: Main webpage.
- `style.css`: Styling and layout.
- `logo.png`: Project logo.
